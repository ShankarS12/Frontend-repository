Niramaan project

Nirmaan Organization, a BITS Pilani alumni-led NGO founded in 2005, is dedicated to building a society where every individual, regardless of their background, is empowered to thrive with equality, opportunity, and compassion. Through our Transformative empowerment programs in Education, Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Health & Well-being, Rural & Community Development, Environmental Sustainability, and Disaster Relief, we are creating opportunities to build an inclusive and sustainable future for all.
